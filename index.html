What is Chronic Kidney Disease?

CKD is a condition where kidneys are damaged and cannot filter blood as well as they should. This leads to waste build-up in the body and other health problems like heart disease and stroke.

  • Stage 1-2: Mild loss of function.
  • Stage 3: Moderate loss of function.
  • Stage 4-5: Severe loss / Kidney failure.

Understanding Chronic Kidney Disease
